Benchmarks

Benchmark sets for VeriMAP, HSF(C), ARMC, TRACER consisting of C programs taken from DAGGER, INVGEN, SV-COMP 13, TRACER.

Reports

  • Gen_M (monovariant generalization with widening only)
  • Gen_MH (monovariant generalization with widening and convex hull)
  • Gen_P (polyvariant generalization with widening only)
  • Gen_PH (polyvariant generalization with widening and convex hull)

Please note that for some programs, marked with (*), the verification time is greater than the considered time limit of five minutes. For some of these programs VeriMAP produced a correct answer.

Programs marked with (*) and programs with result 'unknown' have been considered as timed-out programs in the experimental results presented in the paper.
